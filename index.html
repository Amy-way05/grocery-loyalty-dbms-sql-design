Reward Strategy

Customer Reviews

Measures review participation and connects feedback behavior to customers and tiers.

Approach & Outcomes

The project turns a DBMS assignment into a professional database engineering and analytics case study.

Approach

  • Profiled the original SQLite database and identified 8 core business entities.
  • Rewrote the schema into clean SQL with foreign keys, indexes, and constraints.
  • Grouped analysis queries into loyalty, store, reward, and review decision areas.
  • Excluded raw customer-style data from the public repo while preserving schema and analytical value.

Outcome

The deliverable demonstrates database design, SQL reporting, entity relationship thinking, business interpretation, and data privacy judgment in a format that can be reviewed quickly by hiring managers or technical evaluators.
